Time is not fundamental. Space is not fixed.
Only Entropy is increasing.
The Tsang Entropy-Decay Theory offers a new way to understand how the universe evolves — through irreversible entropy decay, not external forces.
From the birth of galaxies to the behavior of living cells, it views structure, memory, and gravity as outcomes of the same entropic process.

What the Theory Proposes

  • An Entropy-Decay Equation that describes all physical interactions as patterns of resistance and release within entropy itself.
  • A 33-Dimensional Framework mapping the full spectrum of entropic behavior — from stillness to collapse.
  • A view of mass and gravity as effects of trapped internal entropy interacting with cosmic curvature.
  • Predictions that resonant triggers can release stored entropy, producing motion, transformation, or breakdown.
