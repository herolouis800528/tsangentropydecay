The Entropy-Decay Universe:
Redefining Time, Force, and Structure in Modern Physics

This book introduces Tsang’s Entropy-Decay Theory, aiming to reconstruct foundational physics by exploring topics from high school to undergraduate levels.

In The Entropy-Decay Universe, Louis Hin Lok Tsang presents a radical reinterpretation of physics built on a single core idea: that the universe is not a sequence of events in time, but a structured unfolding of entropy itself. Through a unified theory based on entropy gradients, decay progression, and breathing curvature fields, this textbook rebuilds classical and modern physics from the ground up.

Beyond the Standard Model:
An Advanced Study of the Bottlenecks in Modern Science through Higher-Level Entropy-Decay Theory and Equations

In his bold and visionary book, Beyond the Standard Model: An Advanced Study of the Bottlenecks in Modern Science through Higher-Level Entropy-Decay Theory and Equations. Theoretical physicist Louis Hin Lok Tsang challenges the foundations of modern physics—not to dismantle them, but to reconstruct them on a deeper principle: entropy. This is not just another speculative theory; it is a fully developed framework.

In the final chapter of this book, Louis Hin Lok Tsang presents a comprehensive 33-dimensional entropy-decay model. This model reinterprets the structure of physical systems across dimensions ranging from -1D to 32D. It challenges conventional concepts of time, space, mass, and motion by introducing different dimensions of freedom related to irreversible entropic events.
